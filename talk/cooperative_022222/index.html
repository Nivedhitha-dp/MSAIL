Open Problems in Cooperative AI

MSAIL
Slides Video
Credit: Dafoe et al.

Date
Feb 22, 2022 6:00 PM &mdash; 7:00 PM

A recent area of study in AI has been focused on the problem of cooperation amongst machine learning agents. These cooperation problems are widespread, from routine challenges such as driving on highways and working collaboratively, all the way up to global challenges like commerce, peace, and pandemic preparedness. If AI is to play a larger role in society, it is important that AI agents will be able to cooperate effectively with other agents (other AI, humans, etc).
